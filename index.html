Aetherium

Project Aetherium, a placeholder name

A bundle of hopes, dreams and good intentions to make a 2D roguelite RPG with an evolving open world and a natural ecosystem with a main focus on emergent gameplay.

Inspirations

Inspirations are taken from a vast amount of games out there. There are however a couple of games where a circle can be drawn around a certain feature or idea that closely resembles what is envisioned for project Aetherium.

Ultima Online Originally Ultima Online was planned to have a living ecosystem that would work in a somewhat realistic sense. This concept is what we would like to "recreate" for project Aetherium.
Elona Elona is a game I keep going back to. It is a roguelite that has managed to become one of my comfort food games due to multiple "features" it implements. Elona (the original, not the mobile version) will be a BIG influence in the feel of the game.
Dwarf Fortress

Features

  • Evolving open world that does not revolve around the player
  • Intricate magic system
  • Unique Dialogue system
  • offspring-based respawn system
  • Customizable NPC combat behaviours
  • npcs determining their own schedule via hierarchy of needs
  • QoL Belt
