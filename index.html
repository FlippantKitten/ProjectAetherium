Project Aetherium

A 2D roguelite RPG with an evolving open world and a natural ecosystem with a main focus on emergent gameplay.

Inspirations

  • Ultima Online
  • Elona
  • FF XII
  • Rimworld
  • Dwarf fortress

Features

  • Evolving open world that does not revolve around the player
  • Intricate magic system
  • Unique Dialogue system
  • offspring-based respawn system
  • Customizable NPC combat behaviours
  • npcs determining their own schedule via hierarchy of needs
  • QoL Belt

Frequently asked questions

Question Response
Are you even capable of doing this? Probably not.
Are you serious? No, I'm not. But I do realize the size of the undertaking and have decided to go with a "underpromise and overdeliver" type of methodology.
What is the game going to play like? Imagine a top-down 2D RPG in the genre of the SNES era Final Fantasy games. The main difference will be you are not locked to whole numbers on the grid.
What is the game going to look like? See the answer above. As an addendum I'd like to note that all of the assets currently used are placeholders that are royalty-free and thus provide me with a way to convey a general idea with minimal graphical work
